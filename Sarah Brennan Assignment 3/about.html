About the Author

Sarah Brennan

My name is Sarah Brennan. I am 28 years old. I was born in England and lived there until I was 12 when I moved to Cork. I chose to study Computer Science because it was something I have always been interested in. I studied Software development in CIT for 4 years. Unfortunately I found I did not learn much from my experience and did not work in the field. After a few years of working in call centres I decided that I wanted to return to studying Computer Science and I wanted to make sure that there would be the ability to study hardware modules as well as software, which I found when looking up Griffith College.
