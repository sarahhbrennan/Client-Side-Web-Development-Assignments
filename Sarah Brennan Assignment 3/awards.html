Awards and Achievements

List of Awards and Achievements in Chronological Order

  1. 1970: BAFTA Desmond Davis Award
  2. 1972: Royal Geographical Society's Cherry Kearton Medal and Award
  3. 1974: Commander of the Order of the British Empire (CBE) for services to nature conservation in the 1974 Birthday Honours
  4. 1980: BAFTA Fellowship
  5. 1981: Kalinga Prize for the Popularization of Science from UNESCO
  6. 1983: Elected a Fellow of the Royal Society (FRS) under Statute 12
  7. 1985: Knight Bachelor in the 1985 Birthday Honours
  8. 1991: Commander of the Royal Victorian Order (CVO) for producing Queen Elizabeth II's Christmas broadcast for a number of years from 1986 in the 1991 Birthday Honours
  9. 1991: Foreign Honorary Member of the American Academy of Arts and Sciences
  10. 1996: Companion of Honour (CH) for services to nature broadcasting in the 1996 New Year Honours
  11. 1997: Honorary Degree awarded by Ghent University
  12. 1998: International Cosmos Prize
  13. 2000: RSPB Medal
  14. 2003: Michael Faraday Prize awarded by the Royal Society
  15. 2004: Descartes Prize for Outstanding Science Communication Actions
  16. 2004: Caird Medal of the National Maritime Museum
  17. 2004: José Vasconcelos World Award of Education awarded by the World Cultural Council
  18. 2005: Order of Merit (OM)
  19. 2005: Nierenberg Prize for Science in the Public Interest
  20. 2006: National Television Awards Special Recognition Award
  21. 2006: Institute of Ecology and Environmental Management – Institute Medal in recognition of his outstanding contribution to the public perception and understanding of ecology
  22. 2006: The Culture Show British Icon Award
  23. 2007: British Naturalists' Association Peter Scott Memorial Award
  24. 2007: Fellowship of Society of Antiquaries
  25. 2008 The Royal Photographic Society awarded Attenborough its Progress medal and Honorary Fellowship in recognition of any invention, research, publication or other contribution which has resulted in an important advance in the scientific or technological development of photography or imaging in the widest sense.
  26. 2009: Prince of Asturias Award
  27. 2010: Fonseca Prize
  28. 2010: Queensland Museum Medal
  29. 2011: Society for the History of Natural History Founders' Medal
  30. 2011 Association for International Broadcasting AIB International TV Personality of the year
  31. 2012: IUCN Phillips Memorial Medal for outstanding service in international conservation

Source for list: Wikipedia.com
