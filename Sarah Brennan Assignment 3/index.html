Introduction

Sir David Attenborough: OM, CH, RVO, CBE, FRS, FLS, ZSL, SAL

David Attenborough is a successful zoological presenter, narrator and writer. He was born in London in 1926 and started his career in television in 1952. (Wikipedia.com, n.d.) Since then he has presented over 100 documentaries and has even had species named after him. Not only this, Attenborough has also had a huge impact on the environment which he has discussed and brought to light the impact we as humans are having on it. He has been awarded many honours in his time and was even knighted in 1985. These, and many other reasons discussed, are the reasons why I chose to make a tribute website for David Attenborough, my personal "hero".
